- What is the difference between var and let?
    var is function wide and can be redeclared
    let is block wide and cannot be redeclared
- What is the difference between var and const?
    var is function wide, can be redeclared and reassigned
    const is block wide and cannot be redeclared or reassigned
- What is the difference between let and const?
    let can be reassigned
    const cannot be reassigned
- What is hoisting?
    when the variables are moved and declared at the beginning of the execution but not assigned until their original position in the code
